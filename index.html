Erklärung

What is a fraction?

A fraction is used to express ratios or proportions. In short, a fraction represents a part of something.

Let's cut the apple in half.

Definition

Let's take a closer look at the fraction:

A fraction always consists of 3 components:

3
A number above the stroke,
-
a stroke
4
and a number below the stroke.

Numerator

The above number is called the numerator. This number gives you the quantity, the number.

Stroke

The stroke is called a fraction stroke. The dash has the same meaning as a division. ¾ also means 3 : 4

Denominator

The lower number is called the denominator. It represents the whole.

Übung

Example

If you eat about half a pizza, you cut it into two pieces and eat one slice (1/2). If you eat one quarter of a pizza, you cut it into four pieces and eat one slice (1/4).
